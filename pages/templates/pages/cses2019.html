Computational Science and Engineering Symposium 2019

The Centre for Computational Science and Engineering (CCSE) is pleased to invite you the 1st Computational Science and Engineering Symposium. The symposium will be held at the University of Toronto Institute for Aerospace Studies (4925 Dufferin St, Toronto, ON) from May 21—22, 2019. This two-day event provides exciting opportunities to network with experts, stay on top of the latest technological advances, and identify potential alliances and partnering opportunities with international scientists! Registration is required for this event

Call for Papers

We invite researchers to submit their recent work on developments, analysis, or applications in computational science and engineering. Potential topics could be related to machine learning, uncertainty quantification, complexity reduction techniques (e.g. dimensionality reduction), optimization, or partial differential equations.

A submission should take the form of a 1 page extended abstract (single column in at least 12pt font, submitted in PDF format). Author names do not need to be anonymized and references may extend as far as needed beyond the 1 page limit. Submissions may include a supplement/appendix, but reviewers are not responsible for reading any supplementary material.

Please submit abstracts here . Accepted submissions will be invited to present a poster at the symposium. Additionally, the accepted PDF files will be posted on the symposium website. Authors must also register for the symposium

Abstract Submission Deadline: April 8, 2019. 11:59pm EST. Notification to presenters: April 22, 2019

Questions? Please contact Trefor Evans at trefor.evans@mail.utoronto.ca
